Fotokopi

Fotokopi is a cafe ordering system designed to streamline operations at a cafe offering coffee, pastries, and cakes. It includes:

  • Customer Page: Allows customers to add items and complete payments.
  • Admin Page: Helps staff confirm payments and manage orders based on a queue system.

This ongoing degree project addresses staff management challenges, improves workflow efficiency, and business solutions.

In this project, I collaborated as both a developer and tester. While my primary focus was on building and refining the functionality of the system, I also worked extensively on testing to ensure a seamless user experience.

LCK

LCK, or Luq's Chicken Restaurant, is a website made for a chicken restaurant. It features:

  • Customer Page: Enables customers to place orders and complete payments.
  • Admin Page: Assists staff in confirming payments and managing orders in a queue.

This was my diploma project during the final semester, focusing on an e-commerce system for food businesses.

In this project, I acted as the project manager, developer, and tester. I faced challenges in aligning team output with my quality expectations, often stepping in to improve their work. However, my team members excelled in creating documentation, which helped me manage the project despite my own documentation limitations.

Kazoku Sushi

Kazoku Sushi is a staff-oriented application developed using Visual Basic (VB.Net) for a sushi restaurant. It includes:

  • Payment System: Simplifies staff operations for processing customer payments.
  • Booking System: Helps manage reservations efficiently.

This diploma project focuses on enhancing operational efficiency for food businesses.

For this project, I played a significant role in development while the rest of my team worked on detailed documentation and user testing. This collaborative effort helped me with error handling with the application.
